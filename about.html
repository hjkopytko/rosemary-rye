About

My Inspiration for Rosemary & Rye

My daughter, who is the sunshine of my life. This place, Saskatchewan in all of her seasons. My favourite restaurants— such as Flip Eatery (Regina) and Bar Kismet (Halifax)—fuel our vision. Inspired not just by their menus, but by their fearless creativity, the joy they derive from their craft, their passion, and the memorable experiences they create. This journey is about stepping onto my own platform as a chef—moving beyond cooking within someone else's vision to fully embracing my own. Food has always been central to my life: I grew up growing it, matured cooking it, and now, I celebrate it. With a deep passion for ingredients and a personal connection to nourishment, shaped by my marriage to a dietitian, food is more than a profession—it's the heartbeat of my world.
